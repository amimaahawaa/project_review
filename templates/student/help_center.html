Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a project group?

Teachers will assign you to groups. If you haven't been assigned to any group, please contact your teacher or administrator.

What file formats are accepted for submissions?

We accept PDF, DOCX, ZIP, and most common image formats. Maximum file size is 25MB.

How long does it take to get feedback?

Teachers typically provide feedback within 3-5 working days after submission.

Can I resubmit my project?

Yes, you can resubmit if your previous submission was rejected or if you need to make improvements.
